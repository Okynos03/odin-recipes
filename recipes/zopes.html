Bean Zopes

Bean Zopes are typical Mexican food so delicious made with love and corn dough and also beans.

Ingredients:

  • Corn dough to make the zopes
  • Refried beans.
  • Cheese (if you want).

Steps:

  1. Cook the dough and make the form for zopes.
  2. Put the beans on the zope.
  3. Add cheese on if you want.
  4. You can add something like Sauce or avocado if you want.
  5. Enjoy the Zopes.
