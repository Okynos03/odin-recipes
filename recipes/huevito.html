Huevito wiht Sausage

Huevito (egg) whit Sausage is a Universal Dish for Breakfast and also so deliciuos.

As indicated by the name, Huevito with Sausage are a typical but fantastic option for a nutritive and good breakfas.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs (or wherever you want).
  • 2 sausage (the same wherever yuo want).
  • A pinck of salt
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs and chop the sausage into pieces.
  2. Hat the oil and cook the sausage pieces for bit (like 5 minutos).
  3. Then add the beat eggs and cook.
  4. Enjoy your easy breakfast.
