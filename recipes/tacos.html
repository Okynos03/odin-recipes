Tacos

Tacos are probably the most famous Mexican food of the world and for and with a good reason, they are delicious.

For this recipe i want that you to know Tacos de Asada, they are beef tacos marinated in citrus juices and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Skirt steak
  • Chopped onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Corn oil
  • Corn Tortilla

Steps:

  1. Cut the beef and marinate with Mojo Criollo at least per 2 hours or max 24 hours.
  2. Pat the meat dry with paper towels and the cook with oil.
  3. Heat the tortillas.
  4. Finally, build your taco putting the Asada on the tortilla and serve it to your liking.
